---
title: 'Create Patient'
openapi: 'POST /patients'
---

Create a new patient in your OpenLoop Health Partners account. Foundation for patient management and program enrollment.

**Required Fields:**
- Email, first/last name, phone, birth date
- Gender & sex (both required for medical records)
- At least one address location

See [Data Standards](/api-reference/data-standards) for formatting rules and validation patterns.
